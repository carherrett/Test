Welcome to Our Shop: About Us

Welcome to the Bouquet Boutique – for all your flower and plant needs. We are proud to offer you our online catalogue of specialty flowers, plants and gifts. We are certain that you will find the ideal gift to show your appreciation and affection for those special people in your life – or even just for yourself! We offer a wide selection of flowers, handcrafted bouquets, succulents and other indoor house plants. Interested in learning what it takes to create the perfect bouquet? Check out our Calendar page to see our programs we offer – DIY Terrariums, Wine and Bouquet Night, and many more!

We hope you enjoy your online shopping experience with 24-hour ordering right at your fingertips from the comfort of your own home. If preferred, you may call us to place orders at (519) 431-3437 or visit us in store at our location. We are happy you chose to celebrate your special occasions with Bouquet Boutique!
